SignalWire Setup Guide

Follow these five steps to connect your first AI agent to a real phone number in seconds!

1

Create a SignalWire Account

Sign up for a free SignalWire Space. This gives you a dashboard where you'll manage phone numbers and call settings.

Create a SignalWire Space
2

Buy a Phone Number

In your SignalWire Dashboard, go to Phone Numbers and click + Buy a New Phone Number. Pick any local number in your area.

3

Create a SWML Script Resource

Go to Resources, click + Add New, and choose SWML Script. Select "Use an external URL for your SWML Script" and paste the SWML URL you copied above.

4

Assign the Script to Your Phone Number

Go back to Phone Numbers, click your number, then Edit Settings. Under "Handle Calls Using", select "a SWML Script" and pick the script you just created.

5

Call Your Number

Dial your SignalWire phone number from any phone. Buddy should answer and start chatting with you.

Tip: To switch between agent steps, just update the SWML Script resource with a different URL from below. No restart needed.
